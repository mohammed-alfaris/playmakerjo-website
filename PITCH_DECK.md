## Slide 2 — The Problem

Booking a pitch in Jordan is broken.

- **Fragmented**: Venues live on WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and phone calls
- **Opaque pricing**: Rates change by day, time, and who you know
- **No availability visibility**: You call 5 places before finding an open slot
- **Payment friction**: Cash, bank transfer screenshots, trust-based bookings
- **Owners lose revenue**: No-shows, double-bookings, no analytics, empty off-peak hours

> *75%+ of venue bookings in Jordan still happen via direct messaging.*

---

## Slide 3 — The Solution

A **three-sided platform** that digitizes the entire booking lifecycle.

1. **Players** — Discover venues, see real-time availability, book & pay instantly via CliQ
2. **Venue Owners** — Get a dashboard to manage pricing, availability, bookings, and revenue
3. **Admins** — Onboard venues, verify payments, resolve disputes

One app. One source of truth. Instant confirmations.

## Slide 8 — Business Model

**Commission-based, transaction-aligned.**

- **10–15% commission** on every confirmed booking
- **Zero upfront cost** for venue owners — we only earn when they earn
- **Premium tier** (future): Featured listings, advanced analytics — $30–50/mo per venue
- **Payment processing margin**: Small markup on CliQ/Stripe transactions

**Unit economics (target)**
- Avg booking value: 20 JOD (~$28)
- Take rate: 12%
- Contribution per booking: ~$3.40
