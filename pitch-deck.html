The Problem

Booking a pitch in Jordan
is broken.

  • Fragmented: Venues live on WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, and phone calls
  • Opaque pricing: Rates change by day, time, and who you know
  • No availability visibility: You call 5 places before finding an open slot
  • Payment friction: Cash, bank screenshots, trust-based bookings
  • Owners lose revenue: No-shows, double-bookings, zero analytics
75%+ of venue bookings in Jordan still happen via direct messaging.
